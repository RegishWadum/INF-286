Music

Music has, and always will be, an integral part of my life. I've been playing trumpet since the 6th grade (so 14 years. Longer than my sister has been alive!) I also play the flugelhorn, and recently I branched outside of the brass world and purchased a shamisen.

Drum Corps, and the Madison Scouts

This activity deserves its own category for many personal reasons. I have been doing drum corps with the Madison Scouts for 3 years (2019, 2021, and 2022). This activity was so life-changing for me that I don't know where I would be had I not auditioned.

Gaming

I have been playing video games since I was about six years old, and is the reason why I'm in college for computer science! I met many of my lifelong friends through gaming and the internet. Despite us all being in a different part of the world, we always stay connected. Some of my favorite games include the following

  • Halo (I am also a tester for the very first Halo fan game that has ever been released known as Project Contingency! Fun fact: My online alias was inspired by Halo.)
  • Monster Hunter (I tend to hunt solo since I enjoy the challenge of facing massive beasts and dragons by myself)
  • Anything that allows me to explore a vast world like Minecraft or No Man's Sky

Other Cultures

One of my greatest fascinations is learning about other cultures. I want to be immersed in a culture that isn't my own, just to experience what it's like to see how other groups of people are. I also love learning languages. Aside from fluency in English, I am proficient in German. I also understand a bit of Brazilian-Portuguese, Spanish, some Swedish, and Romanian.
